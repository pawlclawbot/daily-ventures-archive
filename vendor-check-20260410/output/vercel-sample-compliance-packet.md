## 6. Risk Gaps From Public-Only Review
A buyer relying only on public materials would still want clarification on:
- formal certifications or audit attestations

- data retention and deletion workflows
- incident response commitments and notification terms
- access control and employee privilege management
- subprocessor disclosures
- backup and disaster recovery posture
- regional data residency options

These are not necessarily failures. They are simply areas where public pages may not provide enough depth for procurement review.
