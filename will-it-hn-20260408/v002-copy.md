## FAQ

**1. How does the scoring work?**
We use a combination of pattern analysis from high-performing HN posts and a model trained on what the HN community actually responds to: technical novelty, founder authenticity, market timing, and the "show don't tell" factor. It's not magic — it's pattern recognition on thousands of posts and the rubric that HN's culture runs on.

**2. Is this actually useful or just a gimmick?**
Fair question. It won't guarantee a front-page hit — nothing will. But if you've ever launched something on HN and watched it die at 3 points, you know that getting the positioning wrong hurts. This tool helps you see your idea through an HN lens *before* you post, so you can decide whether to tweak, wait, or go for it. It's a gut-check with receipts.

**3. What's in the paid tier?**
Unlimited scores, a full category-by-category breakdown (novelty, technical depth, timing, positioning, community fit), and a pre-written Show HN post draft you can use as-is or edit. You also get iteration mode — change your description and re-score instantly to see how framing affects the number.

**4. How many free scores do I get?**
Three per day. Enough to test a few ideas or angles before committing. If you're actively building or have multiple ideas in the hopper, $9/month for unlimited is probably worth it.

**5. Why HN specifically?**
Because HN has a unique culture that doesn't behave like Product Hunt or Reddit. What works there is specific: technical credibility, genuine novelty, honest framing, founder-led narrative. A generic "is this a good idea" tool won't tell you if HN will care. We focus on HN because getting it right there has outsized value — the audience is dense with engineers, VCs, and early adopters who actually build things.
